I've taken lots and lots of pictures in Lincoln Park, Lakeview and around the North Pond for over 15 years. It's a pleasure to make some available to you via and API for your enjoyment, or use them to build your projects. In particular I photographed the North Pond 365 X 360. This means that the pictures are from all angles and all seasons. Enjoy them and share your feedback.

Use a custom search form

For your convenience, I've made available a search form where you can specify the criteria of the photos you're looking for. You must provide the subject of the photo And either the orientation (portrait or landscape) or the season the photo was taken, or both. This search will return at most 10 random images that meet the criteria you specified. You can download and use the photos royalty-free.

Often, I happened to be fortunate to be there at the moment an event is occurring. And like a "chasseur d'image", or a paparazzi if you prefer, I snap some shots to record the event. For example you'll see the series of photos of a male goose standing gard to protect a female goose covering her eggs. Another interesting event was a bird that just killed a frog and now battling to swallow for lunch... In Lincoln Park the statue of Alex Hamilton shines bright.
