About LPLVNP

LPLVNP is a API project that I set off to share my experience living in this part of Chicago for over 25 years through the lenses of my photo camera. I hope you will enjoy these pictures and most importantly, you will use them in your web or publication projects, or just show to other people a glimpse of life in Lincoln Park, Lakeview, and especially round the North Pond
